Below is a guide of EPS buildings as well as utilities at and near EPS:

Parking and Gas EPS and its surrounding area provide students with ample opportunity to both park and get gas. Provided they get permission from the school, they can use EPS-reserved parking spots located on the Northern, Northeastern, and Southeastern ends of campus. There are also underground parking spots inside the TALI building. A popular non-campus parking area for EPS students is the park-and-ride on the other side of NE 38th Pl. The park-and-ride also has an electric vehicle charging station. There is also a gas station moments away away when driving down NE 38th Pl.
