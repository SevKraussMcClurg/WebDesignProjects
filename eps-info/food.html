Food Options at EPS

The LPC food options are very good. There is a good variety of breakfast options such as omelets, scones, muffins, sandwiches, and burritos. There is a different lunch every day (all of which are great) with a vegetarian option. There are also 2 soups every day but other options include sandwiches, the salad bar, and other items available on the far end of the food line. There are also a lot of snacks from chips to ice cream to nuts as well as a bunch of drink options like tea, smoothies, sparkling water, and coffee.

Other food options are the gas station down the street which is a good place to get candy if that's something you want. However, you really aren't supposed to leave campus unless you are a junior or senior so maybe find a friend to go for you. If you are looking for a different lunch option nearby, Burgermaster is on the other side of the block which obviously has burgers and shakes.
